About School


Productivity is never an accident. It is always the result of a commitment to excellence, intelligent planning, and focused effort. With such commitment, meticulous planning and focused effort, the mile stone of GMR SEETHA MAHALAKSHMI DAV PUBLIC SCHOOL was founded. The school was established on 20th January and came into effect on 14th June 2004. The arduous journey was started with a small crew of 146 students and 14 faculty members.
